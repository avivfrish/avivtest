In this experiment you will be asked to decide if a pair is match or not match and to scale your confidence level.
each pair consist from 2 terms from two different schemes.
A Term is a field in schema.
For each term you will be given his: name, type, hierarchy and instances (if exists).
The color in the hierarchy:
Green - Parent of the term in the hierarchy.
Red - The Term.
Blue - Sibling of the term in the hierarchy.

There are three parts at the trail: Logical riddles, first stage, second stage.
