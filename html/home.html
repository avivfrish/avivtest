In this task you will be asked to decide whether pair is a match.
Regarding each pair, you will be decided to scale your confidence level.
Each pair consists of 2 terms from two different schemata, where term is a field in schema.
For each term you might see its: name, type, hierarchy, instances (if exist), system suggestion, and majority decision.
The color legend in the hierarchy:
Green - Parent of the term in the hierarchy.
Red - The Term.
Blue - Sibling of the term in the hierarchy.

There are three parts to the task:
(1) Logical riddles.
(2) Training phase.
(3) Actual task.
